URL:
    /bai4/products?category=chay&limit=10

Controller:
    @RequestParam("category") → biến category
    @RequestParam("limit") → biến limit

ModelMap:
    addAttribute("category", category)
    addAttribute("limit", limit)
    addAttribute("message", "Tim kiem thanh cong")

View:
    /WEB-INF/views/productList.jsp

JSP hiển thị:
    ${category}, ${limit}, ${message}
